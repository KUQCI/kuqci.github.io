Hosted by QCI as part of the IEEE Open Silicon Initiative, this bootcamp brought students into practical chip design through modern IC workflows, open-source silicon tools, reversible logic gate design for quantum computing applications, and collaborative hardware design exercises. Participants developed and tested designs with a path toward Tiny Tapeout submission and fabrication.
