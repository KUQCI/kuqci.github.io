This introductory hackathon opened with a kickoff workshop on 19 January, followed by a week of development across two challenge tracks: a Cybersecurity track rooted in BB84 quantum key distribution concepts, and a Quantum AI track exploring machine learning applications on quantum platforms. The event concluded with final student presentations on 26 January.
